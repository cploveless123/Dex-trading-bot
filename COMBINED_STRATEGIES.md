### Early Stage Recognition (<$10K mcap)

**Green flags:**
- Trade fee spiking >10 in first hour
- Holders growing >50 in first 2h
- Vol/mcap ratio 2-10x (sustainable, not pump-and-dump)
- Top10% <50% (decent distribution forming)
- whale buy signal confirms

**Red flags:**
- Vol/mcap >100x with low liquidity = rug setup
- Top10% >70% = whale/scammer controlling
- BS ratio dropping below 1.0 = distribution phase
- Trade fee flat despite vol = fake volume
